Book Review Analytics

The Book Review Analytics Dashboard uses data analytics and sentiment analysis to provide insights into book reviews, reader opinions, and overall trends. It helps users quickly understand how different books or genres are performing and identify patterns in audience feedback. The dashboard is divided into key pages, including Overview for overall trends, Performance Insights for deeper analysis of factors like genre and publication year, and Future Forecasts with Recommendations to guide strategic decisions. By highlighting the most important charts and visuals from each page, the system makes complex data easy to explore and actionable for authors.

Importance of Book Recommendation

With abundant book choices available, readers often struggle to find books that match their interests. A reliable recommendation system helps users make informed reading decisions.

By analyzing sentiments and ratings, this system improves recommendation accuracy and reader engagement.
